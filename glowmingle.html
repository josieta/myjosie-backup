Glow Mingle App


My role: UI/UX Design Student
Instructor: Quoc Minh Vu, Keyframe Academy

Glow Mingle is a unique social media platform, akin to TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube, but exclusively centered on the beauty and wellness industry. Our goal is to provide reliable and concise information in these fields. The app offers a fresh, calming, and positive user experience with exceptional features that set it apart from other social media platforms. Through the courses, I've strengthened my UI and UX skills and enhanced my teamwork abilities while collaborating with fellow teammates.
